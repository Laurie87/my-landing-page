What is Atomic Beer ?

Because beer is better at home, our company propose you a beer tasting under the same conditions as in a beer shop. But unlike that ... it's us who come to you! ! You just have to book your tasting and indicate the palce where you want to do this great beer experience.

The Founder Of Atomic Beer

Laurie Marthon has been passionate about beer and spirits for years. Wishing to offer tailor-made tastings and a warm climate, it seemed obvious that tasting at home was the best compromise possible.
